Rukudzo Mare

Mare Jnr
Rukudzo Mare known as Mare Jnr is professional soccer player who plays as a winger
or an attacking midfielder for Premier League club Chelsea and the Zimbabwe national team .
He started is career at Derby country ,a team in the championship division in England .
He is also known for his speed ,skill of dribbling the ball and knows how to finish ball well.
Mare Jnr is the first Zimbabwean to win the Premier league golden boot .

Born: April 3, 2000 (age 20 years), Harare, Zimbabwe
Height: 1.63 m
Full name:Rukudzo Sacha Blessed Mare
Parents:M Mare and A Mare
Current teams: Chelsea F.C. (#7 / Midfielder), Zimbabwe national football team (#7 / Midfielder)
